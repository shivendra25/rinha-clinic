Questions

Does this replace my receptionist?

It handles calls and bookings. Your receptionist focuses on in-clinic patients, billing, and operations — the things that actually need a human touch. Many clinics find they need one fewer staff member or can reassign the receptionist to higher-value work.

How does it work with my existing phone number?

We configure call forwarding from your current number to Rinha. Patients dial the same number. The transition is invisible. Takes 30 minutes to set up.

What happens if a patient needs to speak to me directly?

Rinha triages. Routine bookings it handles alone. Complex medical questions or emergencies — it forwards the call to your phone instantly, with a one-line summary of who's calling and why.

Is my patient data safe?

Data is encrypted end-to-end. Stored in India (AWS Mumbai). Compliant with DPDP Act 2023 and IT Act 2000. You control retention. You can delete any patient's data with one click. We never sell or share data.

Can I customize what the AI says?

Yes. Greeting script, FAQs, clinic hours, doctor bio, fees — all configurable through a simple dashboard. Changes take effect immediately.

Does it integrate with my practice management software?

Google Calendar integration is built-in. Practo, Lybrate, and Clinicea integrations are in development. We can sync appointments to any system with a calendar feed.
